Case Study Framework for Word File 
Each case study should follow a structured format to ensure clarity and completeness. 
Title Page 
Case Study Title 
Student's Name 
Course Name 
Date of Submission 
1. Introduction 
Brief background of the case. 
Importance of studying this case. 
2. Case Details 
What happened? (Explain the cyber attack or breach) 
Who was affected? (Companies, individuals, industries) 
How did it happen? (Attack methods used

3. Impact 
Financial loss (if applicable). 
Data stolen or compromised. 
Effects on reputation and customer trust. 
4. How It Was Handled 
How the company/government responded
measures taken to recover from attack
Legal consequesces

5. Lessons Learned 
What security flaws were exploited? 
How could the attack have been prevented? 
Best practices to avoid similar incidents. 
6. Conclusion 
Summary of key points. 
Final thoughts on cybersecurity improvements. 
7. References 
List of sources used for research
